24. 4. They go home on Saturday evening.:(+)(-)(?)​


[Present Simple]

(+) They go home on Saturday evening.

(-) They don't go home on Saturday evening.

(?) Do they go home on Saturday evening?

PEMBAHASAN

Simple Present Tense merupakan bentuk tense yang berguna untuk menyatakan hal yang terjadi sekarang, suatu kebiasaan, serta kebenaran umum.

↬Formula

Positive:

Subject + to be + O / CSubject + Verb 1 + O / C

Negative:

Subject + to be + not + O / CSubject + do/does + not + Verb 1 + O /C

Interrogative:

To be + subject + O / C + ?Do/does + subject + Verb 1 + O / C + ?

↬To Beis: untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggal (He/She/It)am: untuk subjek Iare: untuk subjek You/We/They dan subjek jamak

↬Auxiliarydo: digunakan untuk subjek I/You/We/They dan subjek jamakdoes: digunakan untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggal (He/She/It)

↬Based on Question

Change into negative and interrogative!

(4) They go home on Saturday evening.

Kalimat di atas menunjukkan suatu kebiasaan, di mana 'mereka pulang di hari Sabtu sore.' Maka kalimatnya termasuk jenis present tense. Berikut cara mengubah ke bentuk negatif dan introgatif:

mengubah ke negatif

Letakkan aux/to be + not setelah subjek. Bentuk kalimat ini berguna untuk menyangkal suatu hal.

mengubah ke introgatif

Letakkan aux/to be sebelum subjek dan beri tanda tanya di akhir kalimat. Bentuk kalimat ini berguna untuk menanyakan terkait suatu hal.

(+) They go home on Saturday evening.

Mereka pulang di hari Sabtu sore.

(-) They don't go home on Saturday evening.

Mereka tidak pulang di hari Sabtu sore.

(?) Do they go home on Saturday evening?

Apakah mereka pulang di hari Sabtu sore?

26. whatcan a snowy owls swivel its head?​


Have you ever wondered why owls' heads seem to be on a swivel? It's because their eyes are tubular (like telescopes) rather than spherical, which gives them tremendous vision but very limited ability to move their eyeballs. They rotate their heads to compensate for the fact that their eyes have very limited mobility.
