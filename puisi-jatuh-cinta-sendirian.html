12. THUIZZ!!Banyak susunan kata "sendirian"makan sendirian aja ah..​

sendirian

unsur ganda = 2!.2!

362.880 / 4

= 90.720 susunan kata

s = 1

d = 1

i = 2

r = 1

a = 1

Total huruf = 9

Unsur ganda = n (2), i (2)

P = 9! / 2! × 2!

P = 9 × 8 × 7 × 6 × 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 / 2 × 1 × 2 × 1
