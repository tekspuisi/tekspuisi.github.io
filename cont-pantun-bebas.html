Present Continuous Tense

10 kalimat present continuous tense

Jawaban

Kalimat positif present continuous tense

 I am reading a book now [Aku sedang membaca sebuah buku sekarang]  He is taking a bath at the moment [Dia sedang mandi saat ini]  My mom is baking some bread [Ibuku sedang membuat roti]  They are playing soccer in the field [Mereka sedang bermain sepak bola di lapangan]

Kalimat negatif present continuous tense

 I am not lying [aku tidak sedang berbohong]  She is not yelling at me [Dia tidak sedang berteriak padaku]  They are not listening to their teacher [Mereka tidak sedang mendengarkan guru mereka]

Kalimat interogatif present continuous tense

 Are you lying? [apakah kamu sedang berbohong?]  Are they talking about us? [apakah mereka sedang membicarakan kita?]  Is she cooking for lunch? [apakah dia sedang memasak untuk makan siang?]Pembahasan

Contoh kalimat-kalimat di atas menggunakan pola kalimat present continuous sebagai berikut.

(+ / Positive) Subject + To be(is/am/are) + Verb-ing

(- / Negative) Subject + To be(is/am/are) + not + Verb-ing

(? / Interrogative) Is/Am/Are + Subject + Verb-ing
