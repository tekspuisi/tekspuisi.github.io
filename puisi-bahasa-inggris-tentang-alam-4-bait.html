2. Puisi perumpamaan hewan 4 bait 1 bait 4 baris dalam bahasa inggris


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: Bhs Inggris
Kategori: puisi
Pembahasan:

The Wolves and The Grizzlies

Deep inside the northen woods
beyond those lofty pine trees
there lies the grand goddesses
await for their fair gallant.

Unfortunately, such creatures only exist
in their land of dream. Instead, they are
easily beguiled by the charming wealthy wolves
than by the solvent grizzlies.

In their lovely eyes,
such wolves shall keep their days in bliss eternally
while such brown grizzlies shall
only lead to concord.

The wolves may howl wildly with them at night
but the grizzlies shall stay pure till daylight.
Only time will tell the true righteous one
and they shall be tardy in ruing it.

Ini perbandingan sosok yang menarik tapi licik yang diumpamakan sebagai 'the wolves' dan sosok yang tdk menarik tapi baik yang diumpamakan dgn 'The grizzlies'
