24. besar buga suatu bank adalah 18% / tahun. Besar bunga tabungan firsan selama 5 tahun adalah 375.000 berapakah besar tabungan firsan?


besar bunga 1 tahun = 18/100 x  375.000 = 67.500
besar bunga 5 tahun = 5 x 67.500 = 337.500
jadi besar tabungan firsan = tabungan awal + bunga
                                     = 375.000 + 337.500
                                     = 712.500
B              = 5 x 18 x M                         
                        100
375.000     = 0,9 x M
M              = 375.000 : 0,9
M            = Rp.416.666  
T.akhir   = 416.666 + 375.000 = Rp.791.666

Lavender is a type of flowering plant in the mint family, known for its distinctive purple flowers and sweet, floral scent. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region but is widely cultivated in other parts of the world, including Europe, Australia, and the United States.

The lavender plant is characterized by its narrow, silver-green leaves, and tall, slender stems that can grow up to two feet in height. The flowers are arranged in long, narrow spikes and bloom in shades of purple, ranging from pale lilac to deep violet.

In addition to its ornamental value, lavender has long been used for its aromatic and medicinal properties. The essential oil extracted from the flowers is believed to have calming and relaxing effects, and is commonly used in aromatherapy and herbal remedies. Lavender is also a popular ingredient in perfumes, soaps, and other personal care products.
