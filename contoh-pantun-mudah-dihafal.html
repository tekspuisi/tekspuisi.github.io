4. Contoh teks story telling yang mudah di hafal tentang pahlawan bungkarno


The first President of the Republic of Indonesia, Soekarno, who was usually called Bung Karno, was born in Blitar, East Java, June 6, 1901 and died in Jakarta, June 21, 1970. His father was named Raden Soekemi Sosrodihardjo and his mother Ida Ayu Nyoman Rai. During his life, he had three wives and was blessed with eight children. From Fatmawati's wife, Guntur, Megawati, Rachmawati, Sukmawati and Guruh have children. From his wife Hartini has Taufan and Bayu, while from the wife of Ratna Sari Dewi, a Japanese descendant whose real name is Naoko Nemoto has Kartika's child ...

Soekarno's childhood was only a few years living with his parents in Blitar. During elementary school until graduation, he lived in Surabaya, boarding house in the house of Haji Oemar Said Tokroaminoto, veteran politician founder of Syarikat Islam. Then go to school at HBS (Hoogere Burger School). While studying at HBS, Soekarno had galvanized his nationalistic spirit. After graduating from HBS in 1920, he moved to Bandung and continued to THS (Technische Hoogeschool or High Engineering School which is now an ITB). He won the title "Ir" on May 25, 1926.

Later, he formulated the Marhaenism teaching and founded PNI (Indonesian National Party) on July 4, 1927, with the aim of an Independent Indonesia. As a result, the Dutch included him in Sukamiskin prison, Bandung on December 29, 1929. Eight months later he was tried. In his defense entitled Indonesia Menggugat, he showed the apostasy of the Dutch, a nation that claimed to be more advanced.

His defense made the Dutch even more angry. So that in July 1930, PNI was dissolved. After being released in 1931, Soekarno joined Partindo and at the same time led him. As a result, he was arrested again by the Dutch and exiled to Ende, Flores, in 1933. Four years later he was transferred to Bengkulu.

After a long struggle, Bung Karno and Bung Hatta proclaimed the independence of the Republic of Indonesia on August 17, 1945. In the BPUPKI session on June 1, 1945, Ir.Soekarno proposed an idea about the basis of the state which he called Pancasila. On August 17, 1945, Ir Soekarno and Drs. Mohammad Hatta proclaimed Indonesian independence. In the PPKI session, August 18, 1945, Ir. Bloekarno was elected by acclamation as the first President of the Republic of Indonesia.

Previously, he also succeeded in formulating the Pancasila which later became the basis (ideology) of the Unitary State of the Republic of Indonesia. He tried to unite the archipelago. Even Soekarno tried to gather nations in Asia, Africa, and Latin America with the Asian-African Conference in Bandung in 1955 which later developed into a Non-Aligned Movement.

The G30-S / PKI rebellion gave birth to a great political crisis which caused the MPR's rejection of its responsibility. Instead the MPR appointed Suharto as Acting President. His health continued to deteriorate, which on Sunday, June 21, 1970 he died at the RSPAD. He was buried in Wisma Yaso, Jakarta and buried in Blitar, East Java near the tomb of his mother, Ida Ayu Nyoman Rai. The government conferred it as "The Proclamation Hero
