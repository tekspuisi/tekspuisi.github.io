Rumus Past Continuous Tense

Untuk membentuk kalimatnya, kita harus menerapkan rumus bentuk lampau dari be + present participle (-ing) pada kata kerja utama.
BentukRumus Past Continuous TenseContoh KalimatPositif
(+)Subjek + be (was/were) + verb (-ing) + …She was waiting for you yesterday
They were discussing my birthday party
Negatif
(-)Subjek +be (was/were) + not + verb (-ing) + …She was not waiting for you yesterday
They were not discussing my birthday party
Interrogatif
(?)Be (was/were) + subjek + verb (-ing) + …?Was she waiting for you yesterday?
Were they discussing my birthday party?

7. apa itu simple past dan apa itu continuous? dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban :

- Simple Past -

The simple past tense, sometimes called preterite, is used to talk about an event that was finished at a time before the present. The simple past is the basic form of the past tense in English. Events can be recent in the past or long in the past, and the duration of their occurrence is not important.

- Present Continous Tense -

Present continuous tense is a tense that functions to express an action that is being done in the present (when speaking) and something in the future. The continual present is formed by the auxiliary verb "to be" such as am, is, and is followed by the present participle (V1 + ing).

Kalau Pasangan "to be" nya:

Kalau Verb atau Predikat atau Kata Kerjanya untuk present continuous:

Note: Continuous maksudnya adalah kegiatannya sedang dilakukan.

Ini tinggal tambahin "ing" di belakang kata kerjanya:

ngomong = lagi + ngomong = lagi ngomong-ngomong

speak      =   speak + ing      = speaking

nyanyi = lagi + nyanyi = lagi menyanyi

sing    = sing + ing   = singing

nari       = lagi + nari = lagi menari

dance = dance + ing = dancing

note: ada aturan; bila kata kerja diakhiri huruf "e" maka huruf "e"nya dimusnahkan saja, dan tinggal tambahin "ing" di belakannya (eg: "fire" menjadi "firing", "bake" menjadi "baking")

pengecualian beberapa kata irregular kayak:

mati = lagi + mati = lagi dalam proses mati

die = die + ing = dying - ini dia malah ganti "ie" pada "die" dengan "y"

tinta = lagi + tinta = lagi meninta (mewarnai dengan tinta)

dye = dye + ing = dyeing - ini huruf "e" pada "dye" malah gk dihilangin dan langsung ditambahin "ing", hanya supaya katanya gk jadi sama dengan kata irregular "dying" yang di atas tadi.

Kemudian ada lagi aturan, khusus kata kerja yang huruf belakannya "c"

panik = lagi + panik = lagi panik

panic = panic + ing = panicking

Jadi, perlu ditambahin huruf "k" sebelum "ing" itu.

Dan kemudian ada lagi aturan; Verbs ending with a single vowel plus a consonant.. Ini agak susah aku jelasin sekarang, karena dalam aturan ini, ada banyak variabel yang perlu diperhatikan.

Jawaban Soal:

Maka, untuk jawab soal itu, kita hanya perlu tambahin "to be" dan "verb+ing".

Seperti:

I _____ (fly) in the sky

dijawab:

I am flying in the sky - "am" karena itu to be nya "I", dan "flying" karena "fly+ing"

She _____ (jump) using a trampoline

She is jumping using a trampoline - "is" karena itu to be nya "She", dan "jumping" karena "jump + ing"

Aku lihat semua soal itu mengikuti aturan simple ini aja kok; to be + verb(ing). Jadi "note" aku yang di atas tidak dibutuhkan untuk menjawab soal ini.

35. Jelaskan Tentang Simple present tense dalam bahasa indonesia dan bahasa Inggris! ​


Jawaban:

Simple present tense adalah tenses yang digunakan ketika suatu kejadian sedang berlangsung saat ini atau kejadian yang berlangsung berulang kali (kebiasaan). Tenses ini merupakan tenses yang paling sering digunakan dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Simple present tense is a tense that is used when an event is currently taking place or an event that takes place repeatedly (habit). This tense is the most tense tense often used in English

Simple Present Tense is a verb tense to tell an action happens regularly, or to describe habits and general truth.

(Simple Present Tense adalah kata kerja untuk memberitahu suatu tindakan terjadi secara teratur, atau untuk menggambarkan kebiasaan dan kebenaran umum).

PEMBAHASAN

The Formula of Simple Present Tense (Rumus Simple Present Tense)

(1) Verbal Sentence (Kalimat Verbal)

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + Complement(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + Complement(?) Do/does + S + V1 + Complement?

➡ For Example (Contohnya)

(+) I drink milk every morning. (Saya minum susu setiap pagi)(-) I do not drink milk every morning. (Saya tidak minum susu setiap pagi.(?) Do I drink milk every morning? (Apalah saya minum susu setiap pagi?)

➡ Three sentences above show habits, we can know from adverb of time "every morning."

(Tiga kalimat di atas menunjukkan kebiasaan, kita bisa mengetahuinya dari kata keterangan waktu "setiap pagi").

(2) Nominal Sentence (Kalimat Nomina)

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + Complement (-) S + Be (is/am/are) + not + V1(?) Be (is/am/are) + S + Complement?

➡ For Example (Contohnya)

(+) You are a student. (Kamu adalah seorang murid).(-) You are not a student. (Kamu bukan seorang murid).(?) are you a student. (Apakah kamu adalah seorang murid?)

➡ Three sentences above show about general truth because you are a student in real life.

(Tiga kalimat di atas menunjukkan kebenaran umum karena kamu adalah seorang murid dalam kehidupan nyata).

Note! (Catatan)

Adverb of time (keterangan waktu)

Everyday (setiap hari) Every morning (setiap pagi)Often (sering)Usually (biasanya)Always (selalu)etc. (dll.)

Daily Activity – Simple Present

My name is Sysca Yuliaeni Permana. Some people call me “Sysca”. Now, I’m 20 years old and I live in Jakarta. I live with my parents and my brother. I’m student in Universitas Muhammadiyah Prof Dr Hamka.

I have habitual action that I do every day. This is my daily activities.

Every morning I get up at 5 o’clock, and I go to bathroom to take a bath, I brush my teeth and I clean my body with the soap. Then I take wudhu. After I finish my bathe, I wear clothes and pray shubuh. After that I clean my bedroom, and sweep the floor and the yard. And I have breakfast with my family. After all neat, I drove my mother and my brother to school by motorcycle. Then I back to home. I wash the dishes. After I finished cleaning the house, I watch TV to relax while waiting for 9 hours. I put the make-up on then I go to campus. Because I am student in semester now, I’m free from my college schedule except Monday and Friday. My school begins at half past seven am to eleven p.m. every Friday.

I usually arrive in my campus at 10.00 am. I go straight to library. In library, I open the computer to see some Skripsi on the computer, I feel happy in library because in there I can study and discuss with my friends about our Paper or Skripsi. I usually go home from campus at 05.00 pm after the library closed. After I arrive in home, I clean my foot, then I watch Korean drama on TV. After I hear adzan maghrib, I go straight maghrib praying then I holly quran before study. Then I pray isya. After isya praying, I take dinner, watching TV or relax with my family until 08.00 pm. Then I do my home work and study. After that I go to sleep and so on.

All in all, my daily activity is very happily and I always feel good about my live.

His mother is cooking and his father is taking a bath when Anna calls him. She wants to play with him. Anna asks Adi, “Do you want to play with me?”

Adi answers, “Ok, I am reading now but finish soon.”

Adi and Anna play together. After hours, Adi asks Anna, “Are you hungry?”

Anna replies, “Yes, I am hungry.”

At last, they go to kitchen for eating. Adi does not like chicken. He eats vegetables. Anna eats chicken but she does not like vegetables. They do not like eggs.

Adi’s mother comes and says, “What are you doing here? Don’t eat here.“
