25. Usahakan Ngaca sebelum bertindak !______________6³ + 25 × (2 × 10)​


6³ + 25 × (2 × 10)

= (6 × 6 × 6) + 25 × 20

= 216 + 25 × 20

= 216 + 500

= 716

#BukanMaen

Penyelesaian :

6^3 + 25 × (2 × 10)

= (6 × 6) × 6 + 25 × (2 × 10)

= (36 × 6) + (25 × 20)

= 216 + 500

= 716
