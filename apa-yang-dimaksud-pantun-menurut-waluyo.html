17. perbandingan suhu termometer celcius : reamur = 5 : 4 jika suhu badan waluyo 35 celcius . Suhu badan waluyo jika diukur dengan termometer reamur adalah.... Rcelcius


35° C = 4/5 x 35° R
           = 4 x 7° R
           = 28° RC : R = 5t : 4t 

5t = 35 ===> t = 7

maka: 4t = 4 x 7 = 28

Jadi suhu badan waluyo = 28 R
